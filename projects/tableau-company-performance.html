Tableau – Company Performance Storyboard

Interactive Tableau storyboard analyzing 10+ years of company performance across industries. The project explores trends in sales, profit margins, employees, advertising, and R&D to understand what drives profitability and growth.

Interactive Dashboard

View the live Tableau dashboard: Open on Tableau Public

Key Questions

  • How do sales, employees, advertising, and R&D spending trend over time by industry?
  • Which industries and companies achieve the highest profit margins?
  • How do high-profit and high-sales companies differ from others?
  • Are there geographic patterns in profitability and R&D intensity?

Methods & Skills

  • Created calculated fields (e.g., profit margin, high-sales flag, high-profit sets).
  • Used filters, parameters, and sets to segment companies and compare performance groups.
  • Built multi-page storyboard to guide non-technical viewers through the narrative.
  • Interpreted results to provide strategic recommendations for managers.
